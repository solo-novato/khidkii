The Artist

Hi, I'm Saudamini

Trained in the grammar of space — where walls hold stories and every threshold is a decision. Architecture taught me that structure and feeling are not opposites. They are the same conversation, spoken differently.

I collect details most people walk past. The way light falls at 4pm. The pause before someone speaks. The weight of an unsaid thing. These become paintings.
