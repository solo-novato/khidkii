OUR COLLECTION PHILOSOPHY
Khidki collections are not simply groups of paintings.
They are curated atmosphere systems designed to shape how a space feels, how people interact with it, and ultimately how they remember it.
Each collection explores a distinct emotional landscape through color, narrative, symbolism, and spatial mood.
Together, they create environments that move beyond decoration and become part of the experience itself.
________________


THE SIGNATURE COLLECTION
Sunlit Stories. Familiar Warmth. Places That Feel Like Home.
The Signature Collection is Khidki's most welcoming body of work.
Built around warm ambers, honey golds, terracotta oranges, moss greens and twilight blues, these paintings celebrate moments that feel deeply familiar yet impossible to recreate.
Roads taken slowly. Afternoon silences. Shared laughter. Windows glowing at dusk. The comfort of returning to a place that already knows your story.
The collection moves between landscapes, memory, companionship and reflection, creating spaces that feel approachable, lived-in and emotionally generous.

Each piece feels like a chapter from an unfinished story. A moonlit encounter. A floating city. A forgotten promise. A conversation with the stars.
Rather than depicting places, Storyscape creates emotional landscapes that invite curiosity and interpretation.

EARTH & SILENCE
The Poetry Of Stillness
Earth & Silence is rooted in contemplation.
Inspired by water, forests, journeys and the quiet conversations we have with ourselves, this collection embraces space rather than spectacle.
Every painting feels slower.
Paper Boat.
Yaari.
Bandhani.
Chai Patti.
Deep.
Iceberg.
These are not destinations.
They are pauses.
Built around sea greens, ocean blues, teal waters, forest shadows and softened sunlight, the collection creates a sense of calm without becoming cold.
Small accents of coral, rust and vermilion provide warmth within the stillness.

THE MIDNIGHT COLLECTION
Stories That Surface After Dark
The Midnight Collection explores the emotional architecture of night.
Unlike darkness as absence, these works treat darkness as presence.
Moon And The Window.
Neon.
Selfie.
Tasveer.
Mio Amore.
Mundane Anxiety.
These paintings navigate longing, identity, intimacy and introspection.
Built around deep indigos, charcoal blacks, nocturnal blues and rich emerald shadows, the collection is illuminated by deliberate moments of contrast — crimson, amber, neon pink and electric gold.
The result is a collection that feels cinematic, contemporary and emotionally layered.
