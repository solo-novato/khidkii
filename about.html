Artistic Journey

My creative journey began with childhood doodles that transformed into a lifelong passion. Inspired by the play of light through windows, natural landscapes, and the quiet beauty of everyday moments, I developed a style that blends realism with emotional abstraction. The name "Khidki" (meaning "window" in Hindi) reflects my belief that art opens windows to new perspectives and hidden worlds within ourselves.
